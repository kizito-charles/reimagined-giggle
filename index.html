OUR STORY

Every Kenyan child deserves to master computers

Mtoto Asome Computer is a Nairobi-based initiative inspired by Kenya's "Mtoto Asome" spirit,started by charles ekongo,who is a tech-savvy enthuasiast dedicated to educating young people on digital literacy, in mtoto asome We provide fun, hands-on computer education to children from all backgrounds — whether in schools, community centers, or online.
